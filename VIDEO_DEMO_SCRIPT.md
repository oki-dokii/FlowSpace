## üé§ Voice Over Tips

### Pacing
- Speak clearly but naturally
- Pause for 1-2 seconds after major points
- Let visual moments breathe (don't talk over animations)
- Speed up slightly during setup, slow down for key features

### Tone
- Enthusiastic but not overselling
- Conversational, like explaining to a friend
- Emphasize words like "instant," "real-time," "beautiful"
- Smile while speaking (it shows in your voice!)

### Energy Levels
- High energy in intro (hook them!)
- Steady energy during demo
- Peak energy during real-time collaboration showcase
- Warm, inviting energy in CTA
